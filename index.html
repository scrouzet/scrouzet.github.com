I am a visual scientist, currently working as a Postdoctoral Research Associate with Thomas Serre at Brown University. I will join the lab of Niko Busch at the University of Charité, Berlin, Germany starting next September (2012).

I'm studying the brain mechanisms underlying the recognition of objects in complex visual scenes using neural and behavioral recordings in combination with computational modeling and classification techniques.

I received a master degree in Cognitive Science from the ENS/EHESS/Paris 5/Paris 6/Ecole Polytechnique (Paris, France) in 2006 and a PhD in Neuroscience under the supervision of Simon J. Thorpe (CerCo, Université de Toulouse, France) in 2010.
